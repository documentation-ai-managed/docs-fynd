## Overview

Fynd empowers trail enthusiasts to create interactive trail guides with waypoints, photos, elevation profiles, and real-time updates. You can share these guides publicly or privately, making it easy for hiking groups, tour guides, and solo adventurers to plan safer, more informed outings. Whether documenting a local loop or an epic multi-day trek, Fynd turns your experiences into shareable resources that benefit the community.

<Callout kind="info">
  Fynd focuses on outdoor trails but supports any path-based adventure, from urban walks to mountain summits.
</Callout>

## Who Should Use Fynd

You should use Fynd if you are a hiker, trail runner, mountain biker, or outdoor guide who wants to document routes reliably. National park rangers, adventure bloggers, and hiking clubs benefit from its community-sharing capabilities. Even casual walkers can log daily paths to track fitness progress over time. ## Quick Start

Get up and running with Fynd in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.fynd.com` and sign up with your email or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a New Trail" icon="plus">
    Click "New Guide" and import a GPX file or draw your route on the map.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Details" icon="edit-3">
    Drop waypoints, attach photos, and write descriptions for each segment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="share-2">
    Set visibility (public/private) and share the unique guide URL.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Terminology

Understand these core concepts to maximize Fynd.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Trail Guide" icon="map">
    A complete document containing your route, waypoints, media, and notes. Each guide has a unique shareable URL.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Waypoint" icon="map-pin">
    A marked point of interest along the trail, such as a viewpoint, water source, or trailhead.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Elevation Profile" icon="trending-up">
    A graph showing altitude changes over distance, auto-generated from your GPS data.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Expandable title="Advanced Terms" default-open="false">
  - **GPX Import**: Upload standard GPS exchange files to populate your trail automatically.
  - **Collaborators**: Users invited to edit a guide in real-time.
  - **Webhook**: Optional integration to notify external apps on guide updates (see API docs).
</Expandable>
