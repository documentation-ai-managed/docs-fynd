## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An email address for account verification
- Photos of your trail ready (JPEG or PNG format, under 10MB each)
- Basic trail details: name, location, length, and difficulty

</Callout>

## Create Your Fynd Account

Sign up takes under 2 minutes. Follow these steps to get started.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Fynd" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and go to `https://app.fynd.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and accept the terms.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from Fynd and click it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In and Explore the Dashboard

Once verified, access your personalized dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Navigate to `https://app.fynd.com/login` and enter your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dashboard Tour" icon="layout-dashboard">
    You'll see sections for Trails, Photos, Maps, and Analytics. Use the sidebar to navigate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Pin your favorite trails to the dashboard for quick access later.
</Callout>

## Add Your First Trail Entry

Document your trail details step by step.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web" icon="monitor">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="New Trail" icon="plus">
        Click **New Trail** in the dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Fill Basics" icon="edit-3">
        Enter name (e.g., "Sunny Ridge Trail"), location (`40.7128° N, 74.0060° W`), length (`5.2km`), and difficulty (`Intermediate`).
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open App" icon="app-window">
        Launch the Fynd app on iOS or Android.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Tap +" icon="plus-circle">
        Tap the `+` button and select **Trail**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Upload Photos and Add Notes

Enhance your entry with visuals and descriptions.

````jsx
<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Photos" icon="image">
    Drag and drop up to 10 photos or click **Upload**. Supported formats: JPEG, PNG.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Notes" icon="file-text">
    Write notes like: "Steep climb at 2km mark with panoramic views. Best in spring."
  </Step>
</Steps>
````

## Save and Preview Your Documentation

Finalize and review your work.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Preview" icon="eye">
    Click **Preview** to see your trail as others will—complete with map integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload-cloud">
    Hit **Save & Publish**. Share the unique trail URL: `https://app.fynd.com/trails/sunny-ridge-abc123`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
